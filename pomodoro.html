This Pomodoro Technique boosts focus by using 25-minute intervals of work, separated by short breaks. It prevents burnout and enhances productivity.

STRUCTURE

Ideal For: Focused study, writing, solving problems

Tools: Timer, apps like Forest or Focus Keeper

Time Required: 25 minutes + 5 min break

Complements: Active Recall

Steps to Master Pomodoro

STEP 1

Choose a specific task

Pick exactly what you're going to work on—be specific.

STEP 2

Set a 25-minute timer

Focus solely on your task until the timer rings.

STEP 3

Work with full focus

Ignore distractions. Jot down any that come to mind and return to them later.

STEP 4

Take a 5-minute break

Relax and recharge. Walk, stretch, or rest your eyes.

STEP 5

Take a longer break after 4 Pomodoros

Rest for 15–30 minutes to recover and retain energy.

Congratulations, you have mastered Pomodoro!
